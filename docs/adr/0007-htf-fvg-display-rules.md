# HTF FVG display rules

Slice 3 initially drew only unmitigated 4H/1H FVGs overlapping the Previous Day range. Continuation trading needs overhead gaps (e.g. above PDH) visible as retrace holds, but an unbounded history would clutter native HTF charts. A two-CME-week lookback followed; live execution showed that still carried too much stale imbalance history mid-week.

HTF FVGs on the Structural Canvas follow these rules: all unmitigated 4H/1H wick-based gaps qualify with no PD overlap or liquidity-sweep gate (bullish zone: candle 1 high wick to candle 3 low wick; bearish zone: candle 3 high wick to candle 1 low wick); the middle candle's range must not be fully overlapped by the outer two candles; an FVG does not exist until FVG C3 bar close — intrabar wick conditions on a forming bar do not create or display a gap; formation time is FVG C3 bar open at bar-close confirmation; mitigation begins on the bar after formation; eligibility uses the CME daily session calendar (18:00 ET to 17:00 ET next day, same as PDH/PDL) — gaps formed during the current or immediately previous daily session are eligible for display, Session POI candidacy, Active DOL, and setup logic, expiring at each 18:00 ET daily session roll; on the first daily session of the CME week (Monday 18:00 ET, or Tuesday 18:00 ET after a holiday Monday), eligibility also includes gaps from the prior Friday daily session because no session exists between Friday close and Sunday 18:00 open; boxes render on native 4H and 1H charts only (not projected to execution timeframes); uniform teal unless the gap is Session POI, which gets POI emphasis; mitigation removes a gap when price enters the zone on a bar after formation. Session POI auto-selection at 18:00 ET still only considers gaps that existed at session open — intraday-formed gaps are visible Relevant Levels but do not auto-promote to Session POI. HTF Swing Points keep the two-CME-week lookback; only FVG eligibility tightened to daily sessions.

**Considered options:** PD-range filter only (Slice 3); two-CME-week lookback (prior rule); single previous-day session only (excludes live intraday gaps); two-week lookback for swings and FVGs together; auto-promote intraday FVGs to Session POI when they form in equilibrium.
